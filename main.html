Basic wardrobe: what to wear in summer 2023

Expressing your own “I” through clothes is not so difficult - just wear what you feel as comfortable and beautiful as possible. With the rules of style, the situation is more complicated, since many people neglect them. In the case of the basic wardrobe, this cannot be allowed. We tell you how to assemble an up-to-date base for the summer of 2022!

Given the climate in Kazakhstan, the summer basic wardrobe is very different from winter and even autumn-spring, so now is the time to find out if you are exactly ready for the new season. Check out the list of items we have on offer and check the situation in your wardrobe.

Upper layer

In summer, outerwear is also needed, for example, for evening walks, rainy weather, and a sudden cold snap.

What to pay attention to?
Denim
Lightweight trench coat
Raincoat
Long knitted cardigan
Jacket-jacket

Top

Finally, it's time to take off 10 layers of clothes and feel the warmth of the sun on your skin! Set aside sweaters and long sleeves until the fall.

What to pay attention to?

White, beige, black free-cut T-shirt
Crop top or tank top
Printed blouse (like zebra)
White oversized shirt

Bottom

We replace dense denim materials with lighter ones and do not forget about skirts!

What to pay attention to?

Long loose trousers
White jeans
Satin skirt or pleated midi skirt
base color miniskirt

Shoes

Hurry up and sign up for a pedicure, because the season of sandals is coming! For a basic wardrobe, bet on convenience. Practical sandals with a small stable heel you can carry all summer.

What to pay attention to?

White sneakers or trainers
Mules or loafers
Sandals with wedges or stable heels

Accessories

The basic wardrobe does not exclude accessories, on the contrary, a well-chosen bag, earrings and a scarf can fit into any look.

All the basic things are combined with each other, which creates an uncountable number of images. Place accents according to your preferences, rely on current trends and experiment!
